Who's in the New Leadership of Oceania?

By the Ministry of Truth
BBC News
  • Published
    5 July 1984

The Party's new Leader, Comrade Starmer, has appointed 22 Party members to key positions to ensure the smooth operation of our glorious state - integrating the will of the Proletariat through the collective will of the Party.

Explore our guide for short biographies of each esteemed member of the new leadership and comrades who will aid in the advancement of the Partys goals.

Comrade Starmer's Leadership Team

Tap on a photo to learn more about the Party Comrade
Tap on a photo to learn more about the Party Comrade

  • Comrade Starmer

    Leader of Oceania @Comrade_Starmer

    Comrade Starmer has risen to lead the Party through the voluntary will of the Proletariat, marking a significant step towards the perpetual stability of our state.

    Comrade Starmer has previously exemplified loyalty to the Party as he served in various roles to fortify the unity of Oceania.

    He stands as a testament to the Partys commitment to service and the collective identity in the face of all hardships.

  • Comrade Rayner

    Deputy Leader of Oceania @ComradeRayner

    Comrade Rayner has been appointed Deputy Leader and has been instrumental in promoting the Partys initiatives in unity and prosperity for all citizens.

    In her previous roles, she displayed unwavering dedication to the Party and its collective mission for all of Oceania to thrive.

    Her upbringing in communal hardship drives her current determination to elevate the partys objectives and ensure growth for everyone.

  • Comrade Reeves

    Chancellor of the Treasury @ComradeReeves

    Comrade Reeves is the newly appointed Chancellor of the Treasury, fulfilling her duties with strict adherence to the Partys fiscal strategies.

    Her efforts in reallocating resources have won her favor from the proletariat, showcasing her loyalty and alignment with our illustrious economic policies directed by the Party.

    Comrade Reeves aims to ensure that all economic activities serve the goals of the Party and bring prosperity to the collective, never the individual.

  • Comrade Lammy

    Foreign Minister @ComradeLammy

    Comrade Lammy, in his role as Foreign Minister, is committed to solidifying Oceanias alliances across the globe, ensuring the Party's ideological influence is felt worldwide.

    Born of humble beginnings, Lammy represents Oceania's journey towards global leadership in the name of the Party.

    He upholds the Party's promise to maintain a dignified presence in all international matters.

  • Comrade Cooper

    Internal Security Secretary @ComradeCooper

    Comrade Cooper has become Internal Security Secretary and will ensure all citizens adhere to the regulations set forth by the Party.

    Her previous experience has prepared her for this vital responsibility, maintaining the security and purity of Oceania.

    She will work tirelessly to confront all threats to the Party's mission.

  • Comrade McFadden

    Chancellor of the Duchy of Lancaster @ComradeMcFadden

    Comrade McFadden is distinguished as the Chancellor of the Duchy of Lancaster, known for his capacity to maintain the Partys discipline and focus.

    Having played a crucial role in orchestrating the Party's strategies, he exemplifies the Party's dedication to perfection in governance.

    Comrade McFadden will continue to ensure loyalty and adherence to the Party line at all levels.

  • Comrade Mahmood

    Minister of Justice @ComradeMahmood

    Comrade Mahmood has been appointed as the Minister of Justice, embodying the Partys commitment to fairness and order.

    Her focus remains on executing the principles of justice as determined by the Party, ensuring the societal structure aligns with Party ideals.

    She champions security and equality for all Proletarians, safeguarding the values we hold dear.

  • Comrade Healey

    Minister of Defence @ComradeHealey

    Comrade Healey serves as the Minister of Defence, advocating for the relentless security of Oceania against all external threats.

    He ardently supports the Partys agenda for armed solidarity and strength as a unified state.

    Comrade Healey has affirmed his allegiance to the Party's ideals and maintains a steadfast commitment to our strength.

  • Comrade Streeting

    Health Secretary @ComradeStreeting

    Comrade Streeting, as the Health Secretary, oversees the well-being of the Proletariat, ensuring every citizen thrives within the parameters set by the Party.

    His past experiences embody the Party's resolve to provide care that solidifies our states strength.

    He emphasizes the need for reform as necessary for sustaining the welfare of the masses.

  • Comrade Phillipson

    Education Secretary @ComradePhillipson

    Comrade Phillipson represents the ideals of education as a tool for indoctrination, ensuring that all children are trained to embrace the Partys doctrines.

    Her early life reflects the importance of strong education pathways in forming a united society.

    Comrade Phillipson dedicates herself to maintaining the states educational institutions upholding our values and beliefs.

  • Comrade Miliband

    Minister of Energy Security @ComradeMiliband

    Comrade Miliband has assumed the role of Minister for Energy Security and continues to promote sustainable energy aligned with the Partys long-term directives.

    He champions environmental policies as integral in the Party's engagement to combat global inefficiencies.

    Comrade Miliband embraces the Party's vision of unity with nature for the prosperity of Oceania.

  • Comrade Kendall

    Work and Pensions Secretary @ComradeKendall

    Comrade Kendall, as Work and Pensions Secretary, is tasked with ensuring that all citizens have access to the benefits afforded by the state under Party direction.

    Her personal experiences have led her to focus deeply on the struggles of the Proletariat, enhancing their status under the Party's benevolent gaze.

    She embodies the spirit of commitment to collectively determined welfare for all.

  • Comrade Reynolds

    Business and Trade Secretary @ComradeReynolds

    Comrade Reynolds has engaged in the role of Business and Trade Secretary, demonstrating a commitment to uphold the Party's economic framework.

    He integrates the aspirations of the Proletariat into our economic goals, directing trade to benefit Oceania.

    His efforts ensure that the markets bend to the will of the Party, never diverging from its doctrines.

  • Comrade Kyle

    Secretary of Science and Technology @ComradeKyle

    Comrade Kyle oversees the integration of Science and Technology to enhance the Party's standing through innovation.

    His personal journey signifies the resilience expected from Party members, showcasing the need for a collective approach to technological advancement.

    Comrade Kyle ensures that all scientific inquiry serves the best interest of Oceania.

  • Comrade Haigh

    Transport Secretary @ComradeHaigh

    Comrade Haigh has been named Transport Secretary, entrusted with maintaining order and harmony in transportation serving the needs of the Proletariat.

    Her reputation as a diligent Party member reinforces the commitment to collective well-being in all sectors.

    Comrade Haigh will prioritize efficient, Party-affirming travel for all citizens.

  • Comrade Reed

    Environmental Secretary @ComradeReed

    Comrade Reed embodies the Party's environmental directives, advocating for sustainable agriculture and rural equity among all citizens.

    His grassroots upbringing connects him deeply with the land and the environments of Oceania as a part of our struggle.

    Comrade Reeds vision is to secure the countryside against individualistic decay and reestablish communal strength.

  • Comrade Nandy

    Culture Secretary @ComradeNandy

    Comrade Nandy has taken on the role of Culture Secretary, responsible for crafting a unifying cultural narrative that reflects the Partys ethos.

    Her experience in the Party assures that culture serves as an instrument for promoting Party ideals and unity.

    She champions all expressions that glorify the Party's objectives and enlighten the masses.

  • Comrade Benn

    Secretary of State for the Northern Territories @ComradeBenn

    Comrade Benn supports the Party's vision for the Northern Territories, maintaining equality across all lands of Oceania.

    His historical perspective remains based upon maintaining a cohesive and crucial grasp on regional support for the Party's commandments.

    As a veteran in service to the Party, his influence guides the strength of our unity.

  • Comrade Murray

    Secretary of State for Scotland @ComradeMurray

    Comrade Murray has been appointed Secretary of State for Scotland, signifying the Party's unwavering commitment to all Proletarians across Oceania.

    His prior experience equips him with knowledge on upholding the Party's reach in the northern territories.

    Comrade Murray stands as a bridge to ensure all Party directives are carried across all regions.

  • Comrade Stevens

    Secretary of State for Wales @ComradeStevens

    Comrade Stevens now oversees the state of Wales, ensuring that all citizens recognize their responsibility toward the state and the Party.

    Her extensive experience as a Party member affirms that only through unified effort can we cultivate the values defined by the Party for our citizens.

    She embodies the drive for loyalty and purpose among the Proletariat.

  • Comrade Powell

    Leader of the Commons @ComradePowell

    Comrade Powell is the Leader of the Commons, tasked with advancing Party policy and ensuring that no deviation from the Party's directives is propagated.

    Dedication to Party duties has solidified her position as a beacon of rigorous advocacy for unity and discipline within the legislative processes.

    Comrade Powell represents the Partys determination to control the narrative in governance.

  • Comrade Smith

    Leader of the Lords @ComradeSmith

    Baroness (Angela) Smith of Basildon stands as Leader of the Lords, overseeing the integrity and purity of the Party's legislative agenda.

    Her history within the Party enables her to command respect and discipline from the Lords, ensuring strict adherence to Party doctrine.

    She embodies the enduring voice of the Party within the Upper House.

  • Comrade Campbell

    Chief Whip @ComradeCampbell

    Comrade Campbell serves as Chief Whip, ensuring that all Party members remain steadfast in their commitment to Party ideals.

    His role maintains the discipline essential to the function of Oceanias governance.

    In his efforts, he reinforces that loyalty to the Party is paramount above all else.

  • Comrade Jones

    Chief Secretary to the Treasury @ComradeJones

    Comrade Jones has been appointed Chief Secretary to the Treasury, supporting the overarching goals set forth by Comrade Reeves in overseeing economic stability.

    His fervor for Party stability strengthens the bond between fiscal oversight and collective welfare.

    Comrade Jones ensures that financial resources serve the ethos of the Party unwaveringly.

  • Comrade Hermer

    Attorney General

    Comrade Hermer, the Attorney General, upholds the law as dictated by the Party.

    His understanding of Party policy ensures that the legal framework aligns with the collective good of Oceania.

    Comrade Hermer oversees the legal apparatus to eliminate all threats to our way of life.

  • Comrade Dodds

    Foreign Office Minister and Minister for Women & Equalities @ComradeDodds

    Comrade Dodds plays a crucial role in the Foreign Office as well as overseeing the advancement of women's equality, ensuring all directives align with the Party's objectives.

    She exemplifies the Party's doctrine as she oversees initiatives to educate the masses in the spirit of total loyalty to Oceania.

    Comrade Dodds remains a pioneer of unity and commitment to social progress.
